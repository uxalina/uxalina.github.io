My UX design proccess

UX design process can differ from product to product and brand to brand, and that’s okay. What’s important is to find out exactly what works for each company.

STEP 1

Business research

Business research includes a stakeholder interview to find out the requirements from the business.

STEP 2

User research

User research can include conducting user interviews, questionnaries, surveys, among other methods. Market research implies competitive analysis.

STEP 3

Analisys

Analysis of all data from research and creation of user persona profile, user journey map, user scenarios and user stories.

STEP 4

Idiation & sketching

Brainstorming and generating as many ideas as possible, making rough sketches of ideas, gathering screenshots of ideas in a moodboard and the creation of task flows.

STEP 5

Design

This stage usually includes creating things such as a site map, user flows, low and high-fidelity wireframes and interactive prototypes.

STEP 6

Testing

Testing prototypes with the help of real users, collecting their feedback and refining the design (ideas can be tested at any stage and instantly improved).
